About Me

I’m Margarida Estrela, a photographer from Portugal, drawn to the art of capturing fleeting moments and turning them into lasting stories. My love for photography began in childhood, inspired by my father, who showed me how beauty lives in the smallest details—the quiet play of light, the depth of an unspoken emotion, the elegance of the everyday.

With time, that passion grew, leading me to explore different styles and sharpen my skills through experience and learning. While I pursued a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and now work toward my Master’s, photography remains my creative escape—a way to see the world differently and preserve the moments that would otherwise fade.
